Our Services

What We Provide

  • Medical Department

    Focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and overall care of patients with medical conditions.

  • Surgical Department

    Handles surgical procedures, ranging from minor surgeries to major operations.

  • Emergency Department (ED)

    Provides immediate and critical care for patients with acute injuries.

  • Maternity and Obstetrics Department

    Specializes in the care of expectant mothers, delivery of babies, and postnatal care.

  • Pediatrics Department

    Devoted to the health and well-being of children.

  • Diagnostic and Imaging Department

    Houses services such as radiology, laboratory, and medical imaging.
